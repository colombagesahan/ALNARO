What types of rooms are available at ALNARO RESORT? ‚ñº

ALNARO RESORT offers Deluxe Rooms (semi-luxury, river view, queen bed), King Rooms (spacious, king bed, private balcony, river view), and Opulent Suites (Empire-style, optional rooftop river view, premium furnishings, private lounge).

Do you have facilities for events and functions? ‚ñº

Yes, we have elegant air-conditioned indoor function halls and beautiful garden spaces perfect for weddings, birthdays, anniversaries, and corporate events. We also provide in-house catering and professional staff.

Is there a restaurant at the resort? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Our family restaurant offers authentic, delicious cuisine at reasonable rates, accompanied by a pleasant riverview garden atmosphere. Reservations are recommended, especially on weekends.

Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM and check-out time is 12:00 PM. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and subject to availability and additional charges.

No, ALNARO RESORT does not feature a swimming pool. However, we offer serene river views and a tranquil environment for relaxation.
